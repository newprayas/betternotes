## Quick Guide for Content Editors

### Method 1: Drag & Drop (Recommended)
1. **Select multiple images** on your computer (Ctrl+Click or Cmd+Click)
2. **Drag them all together** over the Images field in Sanity Studio
3. **Drop them** - they'll all upload simultaneously!

### Method 2: Copy & Paste
1. **Copy images** from your computer (Ctrl+C or Cmd+C)
2. **Click in the Images field** and paste (Ctrl+V or Cmd+V)

### Method 3: Individual Upload
1. Click the **"+ Add Image"** button
2. Select images one by one
